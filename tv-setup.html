Bracket Board — TV Setup

2026 Match Play · One-Time Setup Guide

What you need

  1. The Samsung TV outside the golf shop, with the Fire TV Stick plugged in and on Wi-Fi.
  2. The board address: tgcc-brackets.vercel.app

1 · Fire TV Stick

  1. From the Fire TV home screen, search the app store for Fully Kiosk Browser and install it (free).
  2. Open Fully Kiosk and set the Start URL to tgcc-brackets.vercel.app
  3. In Fully Kiosk settings, turn ON: Keep Screen On, Launch on Boot, and Auto Reload on Network Reconnect.
  4. Fire TV Settings → Display & Sounds → Screensaver → Never (or the longest option).

2 · Samsung TV

  1. Settings → General → Power & Energy Saving: turn OFF Auto Power Off and Auto Power Saving.
  2. Turn OFF Eco / Brightness Optimization (it dims the screen).
  3. Picture → Picture Size → Fit to Screen / Just Scan so nothing is cropped.
  4. Optional: set the TV's On Timer / Off Timer to match shop hours — everything restarts itself.

Entering results

  1. On your phone or computer, go to tgcc-brackets.vercel.app/admin and enter the admin PIN.
  2. Pick the bracket, tap the winner's name, type the score (e.g. 3&2, 1 UP), and save.
  3. The TV updates itself within a minute — no need to touch it.
  4. Made a mistake? Tap either player in that match again to change the winner or clear the result.

Handy addresses

  1. TV board (rotates all brackets): tgcc-brackets.vercel.app
  2. Pin one bracket (finals day): tgcc-brackets.vercel.app/?bracket=palmer
  3. Results entry: tgcc-brackets.vercel.app/admin
The board rotates through all five pages on its own, checks for new results every 45 seconds, and reloads itself nightly. If the screen ever looks stuck, pull the Fire TV Stick's power for ten seconds and plug it back in — it will boot straight back to the brackets.
